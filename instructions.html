How to Play

  1. Enter your guess for the Pokémon's name in the input field. You will have ten seconds to submit your guess.
  2. Press the "Enter" key to submit your guess.
  3. If your guess is correct, the Pokémon's image and details will be revealed.
  4. If your guess is incorrect, try again until you get it right.
  5. If the time is over, the Pokémon will be automatically revealed.
  6. You can also reveal the Pokémon by using reveal button.
  7. Keep track of your current and highest streaks displayed on the screen.
  8. Reach a streak of 50 to earn the "Ace" badge.
  9. Reach a streak of 100 to earn the "Master" badge.
  10. Have fun and try to guess em all!
